By bringing together all types of information about species distributions, providing model-based integration, and providing a system for users build upon our knowledge, the Map of Life project hopes to support our community in understanding and saving the world's biodiversity. more about MOL
The end result will be a knowledge-base of initially tens and ultimately hundreds of thousands of species distribution maps, along with a set of tools for querying, accessing, modifying, downloading and summarizing them.
